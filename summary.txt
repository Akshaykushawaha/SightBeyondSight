This article discusses the things to avoid saying to someone who is dealing with anxiety and offers advice on what to say instead. It emphasizes the importance of validation, reminding the person that it is okay to feel scared and that they are not alone. It also suggests methods to help the person calm down, such as deep breathing exercises, and encourages people to share their experiences rather than their expertise. Finally, it reminds us that it is important to be loving and accepting of people who are struggling with anxiety.
